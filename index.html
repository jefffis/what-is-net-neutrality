Dear anyone who doesn’t really know what Net Neutrality is,

Let’s break down what it is and what it is not. Simple.

It is: unrestricted access to all content on the internet for all people; black, white, hispanic, rich, poor, smart, less-smart, techy, non-techy, nerd, jock, romantic, etc.

It is not: government regulation of access to content on the internet.

It is: equally important for everyone; be them Democrat, Republican, Independent, indifferent, insipid, intolerant, or introvert.

It is not: anything about Obama, Obamacare, or any Obama-related issues.

It is: an ability for the small guy to take the big guy down.

It is not: anything but an attempt by large corporations to squeeze even more money out of us.

It is something we should all understand. It WILL affect us all; and most likely all of mankind; that’s how important the free internet is.

If Net Neutrality fails, ISPs (like Comcast, Verizon, and Time Warner, amongst others) will make their own rules. Think you pay a lot for spotty internet access now? This is nothing compared to what will happen if they start charging for access to certain website content.

Do you like Facebook, or Twitter, or Instagram? Well, without Net Neutrality, we wouldn’t know what they are, and we won’t meet the next one. Ever.

So, please, leave your partisan issues at the door, and think about whether you benefit from free access to any website you wish; or whether you think it’s okay for your internet provider to tell you which ones are okay to visit.

Thanks,
A few hundred million concerned citizens
